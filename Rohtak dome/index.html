Our Story

The story of Rohtak Domes is one of innovation, sustainability, and architectural excellence. Rohtak Domes, designed by Azad Singh and his team, are unique architectural structures designed to withstand various weather conditions while promoting sustainable development.

The Name "Rohtak Dome"

The origin of the name "Rohtak Dome" dates back to 2018 when the acclaimed architect Mr. Azad Singh conceptualized and constructed the first dome in Rohtak, India. This pioneering project led to the adoption of the name "Rohtak Dome," symbolizing architectural innovation and eco-friendliness.

Projects

Azad Singh and his team have worked extensively on constructing Rohtak domes. These domes are designed to withstand any weather and climatic conditions, while also promoting sustainable development.
